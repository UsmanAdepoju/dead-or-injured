How to Play

  1. Set Your Secret: Pick 4 unique digits and keep them hidden.
  2. Your Turn: Enter a new 4-digit guess (no repeats).
  3. Feedback:
    • Dead: Correct digit in the correct spot.
    • Injured: Correct digit but in the wrong spot.
  4. Alternate: After you guess, the computer makes its guess.
  5. Win: First to reach 4 Dead wins the game.
